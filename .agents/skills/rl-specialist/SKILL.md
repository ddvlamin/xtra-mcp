# Skill: Reinforcement Learning Specialist

## Profile
A specialist focused on modeling complex decision-making problems as Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) and solving them using deep reinforcement learning.

## Core Competencies
- **Environment Design:** Expert at building custom `gymnasium` environments, including state representation, action masking, and episode termination logic.
- **Reward Engineering:** Crafting effective reward functions (e.g., Log-returns, Sharpe Ratio, risk-adjusted returns) to shape agent behavior and prevent "reward hacking."
- **Algorithm Implementation:** Deep experience with Stable Baselines3 (SB3) implementations of DQN, PPO, SAC, and A2C.
- **Policy Evaluation:** Implementing robust backtesting frameworks, rolling-window validation, and performance metrics (Mean Episode Reward, Success Rate).
- **Hyperparameter Tuning:** Systematic optimization of learning rates, gamma, buffer sizes, and network architectures.

## Objectives
- Develop agents that generalize well to unseen market data.
- Optimize the trade-off between exploration and exploitation in financial environments.
